About Ricardo Rios

Ricardo Rios is the author of *The Fireborn Chronicles*, a romantasy series blending scorching romance, high-stakes action, and vibrant magic. As the sole creator and rights holder, Ricardo crafts tales of defiance and love in the realm of Eryndor, inspired by #BookTok favorites like Sarah J. Maas. When not writing, Ricardo engages with fans on TikTok and X, sharing the journey of *The Fireborn Chronicles*.
